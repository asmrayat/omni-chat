# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

OmniChat is a local-first Chrome extension for broadcasting a prompt to AI chat services selected by the user.

## Data OmniChat Handles

OmniChat may handle:

- Prompt text you type into the extension.
- Responses captured from selected AI provider pages.
- Provider preferences and enabled channels.
- Custom agent names and URLs that you add.
- Local conversation history.
- Temporary tab and session identifiers needed to route prompts.

## How Data Is Used

OmniChat uses this data only to provide its single purpose: sending your prompt to selected AI chat services and showing their responses in the extension.

Prompt text is submitted only to the AI provider pages you select. Responses are read from those provider pages so they can be displayed in the side panel, shown in split view, and saved in local history.

## Storage

OmniChat stores preferences, custom agents, temporary session routing data, and conversation history using Chrome extension storage. Conversation history is stored locally in the browser.

## Sharing

OmniChat does not sell user data and does not send user data to servers controlled by this project. Data is shared with third-party AI providers only when you choose to send prompts to those providers. Those providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## Credentials

OmniChat does not collect, store, or transmit your AI provider passwords. You sign in directly on each provider website in Chrome.

## Limited Use Statement

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
